What are our most popular positions?

Napoleon cake

Delicate, fragrant, the true taste of childhood. Napoleon is one of our customers' most favorite desserts, and for good reason. We prepare it according to a traditional recipe, using only natural butter, fresh eggs and real vanilla. Each layer of thin dough is baked separately to achieve the perfect crispy texture, and the custard is cooked by hand to the desired thickness and tenderness. This dessert goes perfectly with tea or coffee and leaves no one indifferent. It can be given as a gift, served on a festive table or simply eaten with a spoon on a quiet evening. Napoleon is not just sweet, it is a warm memory, a cozy moment and delicious pleasure in each layer. That is why it is always among the top of our orders.

Pavlova

This dessert impresses not only with its taste, but also with its appearance. The base is light, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside meringue, decorated with fresh strawberries and a mascarpone-based cream. Pavlova is a dessert with character: crispy, tender, refreshing. We add a drop of vanilla and a little lemon juice to the cream to make the balance of taste perfect. It is not heavy, not too sweet, and therefore suitable even for those who do not like traditional cakes. This dessert is perfect for holidays, photo shoots and unforgettable evenings with loved ones. Pavlova is like a light summer breeze on your table, you want to not just eat it, but savor every bite slowly, with pleasure.

Apple tart

This dessert is a true autumn classic that never loses its relevance at any time of the year. Crispy shortcrust pastry, caramelized apple filling, fragrant cinnamon and a light creamy touch on top are the perfect combination for cozy evenings. We use only fresh Granny Smith or Gala apples, which hold their shape when baked and have a pleasant sourness. Everything is prepared by hand - from the dough to the caramel, which we cook over an open fire. The tart is especially popular among those who like something "homemade, but with a twist". It goes well with a scoop of ice cream or just a cup of tea. This is a dessert that evokes nostalgia and warms the soul - which is why it is among our most popular items.
